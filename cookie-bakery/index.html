— Our Story —

Baked With Passion
Since Day One

COOKIE started with a simple dream: to create pastries so good, they'd make people close their eyes and smile with every bite. What began as a tiny kitchen experiment between two friends became a beloved neighborhood bakery that now serves thousands of happy customers.

Every morning, our team of passionate bakers arrives before sunrise. The aroma of fresh butter melting into golden croissants, the rich scent of Belgian chocolate being tempered, the warmth of ovens bringing dough to life — this is the magic that happens behind our doors.
